I am Mark Zucker Burg

I've spent my life immersed in the world of technology and innovation. From an early age, I was captivated by the potential of the digital landscape. This fascination led me to embark on a journey that ultimately culminated in the creation of Facebook. My passion for connecting people and fostering communities has been the driving force behind this endeavor. I believe that in an increasingly interconnected world, meaningful connections are at the core of progress and understanding. Facebook emerged as a platform to facilitate these connections on an unprecedented scale. Throughout this journey, I've been dedicated to pushing the boundaries of what technology can achieve, while also being acutely aware of the responsibilities that come with it. The mission has always been to make the world more open and connected, allowing individuals to share their stories, ideas, and experiences with a global audience. But the story is far from over. I remain committed to innovation and to finding new ways to leverage technology for the betterment of society. It's a privilege to be part of this ongoing revolution, and I'm grateful for the opportunities it continues to present. Together, we're shaping the digital landscape in ways that hold the potential to impact lives across the globe.
